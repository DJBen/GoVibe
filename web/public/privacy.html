Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

GoVibe ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the GoVibe iOS app and GoVibe Host macOS companion collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Collect

GoVibe is designed to minimize data collection. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — When you sign in with Apple, we receive an anonymous relay identifier and, only if you choose to share it, your name and email address. We do not receive your Apple ID password.
  • Push notification tokens — We store your device push token to deliver AI agent notifications. Tokens are deleted when you sign out.
  • Connection metadata — Timestamps and anonymized connection identifiers are logged to maintain the relay service. These logs are rotated and deleted within 30 days.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or have access to the content of your terminal sessions, screen streams, or code.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or third-party trackers in the app.

End-to-End Encryption

All communication between GoVibe Host on your Mac and the GoVibe app on your iPhone is encrypted end-to-end. The relay server facilitates the connection but cannot read the content of your sessions.

Data Storage & Security

Account data and push tokens are stored in Google Cloud Firestore with access restricted by security rules. All data is transmitted over TLS-encrypted connections.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app. Upon account deletion, all stored data (account info, push tokens, and connection metadata) is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

GoVibe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
